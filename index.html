ABOUT US

Where the Earth Meets the Tide

Welcome to a coastal sanctuary where tradition, luxury, and the rhythmic pulse of the ocean converge. Kamayan Beach Resort offers an unparalleled escape from the digital noise, inviting you to rediscover a world where life is felt through the sand between your toes and the salt on your skin.

ROOMS

Hand Picked Rooms

Standard Room (The "Essentials" Stay)

The most budget-friendly option, usually located in a main building or a row of concrete units slightly back from the shore.

P499/night

Deluxe Room (The Mid-Tier Upgrade)

Slightly more spacious than the Standard, often with better views or closer proximity to the pool/beach.

P699/night

Family Room / Barkada Suite

Since Filipino travel culture is very group-oriented, entry-level resorts almost always have "Barkada" (peer group) rooms.

P799-P999/night
